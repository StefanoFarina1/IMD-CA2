Brain Disorder Schizophrenia

My project focuses on addressing Brain Disorder Schizophrenia, utilizing software applications such as OpenMRS and OpenEMR. In this endeavor, I engage in creating and deleting patient records, adding patient appointments, generating reports, and incorporating allergies and medications into patient records.

Difficulties encountered
  • One challenge involved difficulties in deleting patient records.
  • Another hurdle was encountered when creating comprehensive reports.
  • Sharing patient records with other users posed additional challenges.
  • Addition of allergies and medications to patient records also presented complexities.

Despite these obstacles, I successfully resolved the issues by leveraging resources such as YouTube videos, support forums related to OpenMRS and OpenEMR software applications, extensive internet searches, and the download of multiple versions of the software.

Car Reviews Website

As part of a collaborative effort, I contributed to the development of a sophisticated car reviews website designed to empower users with valuable insights into their desired vehicles. The platform provides a comprehensive experience, allowing users to access detailed reviews, high-quality photos, ratings, and user comments to facilitate informed decision-making in their car purchasing journey.

Difficulties Encountered
  • Navigating the challenge of designing an aesthetically pleasing website that caters to a diverse user base.
  • Addressing the complexity of implementing robust security measures to ensure user data safety and website integrity.
  • Enhancing user engagement posed challenges, requiring the creation of intuitive and user-friendly features.
  • Integrating a responsive design to ensure seamless accessibility across various devices presented technical hurdles.

Overcoming these obstacles demanded a proactive approach, leveraging resources such as YouTube videos, support forums related to web development, extensive internet searches, and iterative software versions. This dedicated problem-solving mindset enabled the project team to successfully navigate challenges and deliver a robust and user-friendly car reviews website.
